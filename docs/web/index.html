How it works

Three steps to get anything done

Whether you need help or want to earn extra income, Taskly makes the whole process simple and safe.

1

Post your task

Describe what needs doing, set your budget, pick a category. Takes under a minute. No account needed to browse — sign up only when you're ready to post.

2

Get offers & chat

Nearby taskers send you their price and a message. Read their reviews, chat in the app, and pick the person you're most comfortable with.

3

Pay securely, release when done

Pay through the app — funds are held in escrow until you confirm the job is complete. Your money is protected every step of the way.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Taskly is built for trust and simplicity. We handle the boring stuff so you can focus on the task.

Escrow Payments

Your payment is held safely until the job is confirmed done — protecting both posters and taskers.

Real-time Chat

Message before you commit. Agree on the details, share photos, and keep everything in one thread.

Ratings & Reviews

After every job, both sides leave a review. Transparent reputations mean you know exactly who you're working with.

Dual Identity

One account, two roles. Post tasks when you need help, and pick up jobs when you want to earn — switch anytime.

Wallet & Withdrawals

Your earnings land in your Taskly wallet. Check your balance and transaction history, then withdraw whenever you like.

Identity Verified

Taskers can get identity-verified. Look for the verified badge for extra peace of mind when hiring.
